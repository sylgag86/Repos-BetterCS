About National Funding

Founded in 1999, National Funding has established itself as a leading alternative lender for small businesses across the United States. With over two decades of experience and having provided more than $4 billion in funding to thousands of businesses, National Funding has built a reputation for accessibility and efficiency in a lending landscape that often presents significant challenges for small business owners. Unlike traditional financial institutions that may have rigid qualification requirements, National Funding takes a more flexible approach to evaluating business financing applications, looking beyond just credit scores to consider the overall health and potential of the business.

What distinguishes National Funding in the competitive alternative lending market is their comprehensive suite of financing solutions designed specifically for small businesses. Their working capital loans provide quick access to funds for operational expenses, inventory purchases, or growth opportunities with loan amounts typically ranging from $5,000 to $500,000. Their equipment financing options allow businesses to acquire essential machinery or technology with flexible terms and potentially tax-advantageous structures. Additionally, for businesses with substantial credit card sales, their merchant cash advance product offers an alternative funding model based on future receivables rather than fixed monthly payments, providing flexibility for businesses with fluctuating revenue patterns.

National Funding's commitment to streamlining the financing process directly addresses the time constraints faced by busy business owners. Their application can typically be completed in minutes, with minimal documentation requirements compared to conventional bank loans. Decisions are often provided within hours rather than weeks, and approved applicants can receive funding in as little as 24 hours—a critical advantage for businesses facing time-sensitive opportunities or challenges. While their interest rates may be higher than traditional bank loans, reflecting the increased accessibility and speed, National Funding offers early payoff discounts that can significantly reduce the overall cost for borrowers who are able to repay ahead of schedule. For small business owners who value simplicity, speed, and service in their financing partner, National Funding presents a compelling alternative to conventional lending channels.
