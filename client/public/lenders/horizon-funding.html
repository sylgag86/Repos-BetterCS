About Horizon Funding

Founded in 2012, Horizon Funding has established itself as a responsive alternative lender focused on addressing the financing needs of small to mid-sized businesses that often find themselves underserved by traditional banking institutions. The company was built upon recognizing the significant gap in the market: while many small businesses demonstrate strong operational performance and growth potential, they frequently encounter obstacles in securing timely capital through conventional channels due to stringent credit requirements, excessive documentation demands, or extended approval timelines. Horizon Funding's business model centers on providing accessible financing with streamlined application processes, flexible qualification criteria, and expedited funding timelines that align with the urgent capital needs many businesses face. Their approach emphasizes evaluating the overall health and trajectory of a business rather than relying exclusively on credit scores or extensive operating history.

What distinguishes Horizon Funding in the alternative lending landscape is their commitment to relationship-based financing combined with technological efficiency. Unlike many online lenders that operate through fully automated approval systems with minimal human oversight, Horizon pairs digital convenience with dedicated funding specialists who work directly with business owners to understand their specific circumstances and tailor financing solutions accordingly. This approach enables them to consider qualitative factors about a business that may not be captured in standard financial metrics. Additionally, their willingness to fund a diverse range of equipment types and business purposes—including specialized machinery, vehicle fleets, technology infrastructure, and expansion projects—makes them a versatile funding partner capable of supporting various business needs. Their in-house underwriting process (rather than simply brokering loans to third-party lenders) gives them greater flexibility in structuring deals and maintaining consistent communication throughout the approval and funding process.

Horizon Funding offers several core financing products tailored to various business needs. Their equipment financing program provides funding from $10,000 to $250,000 for new or used equipment purchases, with terms ranging from 2 to 5 years and competitive rates based on business profile and equipment type. Their working capital loans range from $25,000 to $450,000 with terms typically from 6 to 18 months, designed to help businesses manage seasonality, pursue growth opportunities, or bridge temporary cash flow gaps. For businesses looking to refinance existing equipment or leverage assets already owned, they offer equipment refinancing options that can unlock capital tied up in paid-off or partially paid equipment. Approval decisions typically arrive within 24 hours of application submission, with funding following in as little as 2-3 business days after documentation completion. To qualify, businesses generally need to have been operating for at least one year and generate minimum annual revenue of $150,000, with credit score requirements more flexible than traditional lenders—typically accepting scores as low as 600 when other business factors demonstrate strength.
