About Peerform

Founded in 2010 by Wall Street executives, Peerform operates as a peer-to-peer lending marketplace that connects borrowers seeking personal loans with investors looking to fund those loans. Unlike traditional banks that use deposits to fund loans, Peerform's platform allows individual and institutional investors to directly fund loans based on their risk preferences and return expectations. This marketplace model enables Peerform to offer loans to a broader range of borrowers, including those who might not qualify for bank loans due to modest credit scores or limited credit history. Their proprietary underwriting algorithm, the Loan Analyzer, evaluates loan applications using multiple data points beyond just credit scores, creating a more nuanced assessment of borrower risk and potentially offering more favorable terms to borrowers who might be underserved by conventional financial institutions.

What distinguishes Peerform in the personal loan landscape is their focus on transparency and accessibility. Their online application process is designed to be straightforward, with pre-qualification available using a soft credit pull that doesn't affect the applicant's credit score. Once approved, loans are listed on their marketplace where investors can review and fund them, typically within 1-3 days. Peerform offers fixed interest rates that remain consistent throughout the loan term, providing borrowers with predictable monthly payments and clarity about their total repayment amount. Additionally, they don't charge prepayment penalties, allowing borrowers to pay off their loans early without additional costs—a significant advantage for those looking to reduce their overall interest expenses.

Peerform offers personal loans ranging from $4,000 to $25,000 with terms of 36 or 60 months, serving various purposes including debt consolidation, home improvements, major purchases, and unexpected expenses. Their marketplace model often enables them to serve borrowers with credit scores as low as 600, making them more accessible than many traditional banks. While their funding process may take slightly longer than some online lenders due to the investor-matching component, their competitive rates and flexible eligibility requirements make them a viable option for borrowers who may not fit the strict criteria of conventional lending institutions. For individuals seeking an alternative to traditional bank loans, especially those with fair credit scores looking for competitive rates and transparent terms, Peerform's peer-to-peer marketplace offers a technology-driven approach to personal lending with potentially more favorable terms than they might find elsewhere.
