About Balboa Capital

Founded in 1988, Balboa Capital has established itself as a leading provider of equipment financing and leasing solutions for businesses across the United States. With over three decades of experience, the company has developed a reputation for fast, responsive service and a streamlined application process that minimizes paperwork and reduces approval times compared to traditional financing channels. Balboa Capital serves businesses of all sizes, from small operations to large enterprises, across various industries including manufacturing, construction, transportation, healthcare, and information technology. Their longevity in the equipment financing marketplace has allowed them to develop specialized expertise across different equipment types and industry requirements, creating a depth of knowledge that benefits their clients.

What sets Balboa Capital apart in the equipment financing space is their emphasis on speed and efficiency without sacrificing personalized service. Their online application process can be completed in minutes, with same-day approval decisions often possible for qualifying applicants. Once approved, funding can be arranged quickly, allowing businesses to move forward with equipment acquisitions without unnecessary delays. Balboa Capital also offers a vendor financing program that enables equipment vendors and dealers to provide financing options directly to their customers, creating a seamless purchasing experience. Additionally, they provide specialized guidance on Section 179 tax incentives, helping businesses maximize potential tax deductions associated with equipment purchases—a significant value-add that can substantially improve the economics of equipment investments.

Balboa Capital offers equipment financing options from $3,000 to $1 million, with terms typically ranging from 24 to 60 months, providing flexibility to align with different budget constraints and equipment lifecycles. Their application requirements are designed to be straightforward, with minimal documentation needed compared to conventional financing sources, particularly for requests under certain thresholds. For businesses looking to acquire new equipment to drive growth, replace aging assets to improve efficiency, or strategically time equipment investments to leverage tax benefits, Balboa Capital offers a combination of speed, expertise, and flexible financing structures. Their focus on creating a positive customer experience has earned them consistently high satisfaction ratings and a substantial base of repeat clients who return for subsequent equipment financing needs.
