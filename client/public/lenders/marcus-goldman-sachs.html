About Marcus by Goldman Sachs

Launched in 2016, Marcus by Goldman Sachs represents the prestigious investment bank's strategic expansion into consumer banking. Named after Marcus Goldman, one of the firm's founders, this digital banking platform combines Goldman Sachs' 150+ years of financial expertise with modern technology to offer accessible financial products to everyday consumers. Marcus quickly established itself as a competitive force in the personal loan space by offering a transparent, customer-centric alternative to traditional banking options.

Marcus personal loans range from $3,500 to $40,000 with terms from 36 to 72 months. What truly distinguishes Marcus is their commitment to a completely fee-free experience—no origination fees, no late fees, no prepayment penalties, and no hidden charges of any kind. Their interest rates are competitive, typically ranging from 6.99% to 19.99% APR for qualified borrowers. Marcus also offers unique features that benefit responsible borrowers, such as their "on-time payment reward" that allows customers to defer one monthly payment (interest-free) after making 12 consecutive on-time payments.

The application process is straightforward and entirely digital, allowing prospective borrowers to check their rate without impacting their credit score through a soft credit inquiry. Once approved, funds can be deposited directly into the borrower's bank account within 1-3 business days. Marcus has received consistently high customer satisfaction ratings, particularly for their U.S.-based customer service team, which is available seven days a week. While Marcus requires a relatively good credit score (typically 660 or higher), they consider multiple factors beyond just credit scores, including income and debt-to-income ratio. For those who qualify, Marcus delivers a premium lending experience with the stability and security that comes from a financial institution with Goldman Sachs' pedigree.
