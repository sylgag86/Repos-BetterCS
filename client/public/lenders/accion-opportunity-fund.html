About Accion Opportunity Fund

Accion Opportunity Fund is a leading nonprofit Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI) formed by the merger of Accion U.S. Network and Opportunity Fund in 2020. With a history spanning over two decades, this organization is dedicated to creating an inclusive financial system that helps small businesses—particularly those owned by women, people of color, and immigrants—access affordable capital, coaching, and networks essential for business growth. Unlike traditional lenders focused primarily on profit, Accion Opportunity Fund's mission-driven approach means they can provide funding solutions to entrepreneurs who might be overlooked by conventional financial institutions due to limited credit history, smaller capital needs, or other barriers.

What distinguishes Accion Opportunity Fund in the small business lending landscape is their comprehensive approach to supporting entrepreneurs. Beyond providing loans, they offer extensive business coaching and educational resources designed to help business owners build financial management skills, strengthen operations, and navigate growth challenges. Their personalized approach means loan officers and business advisors take time to understand each business's unique situation, offering tailored guidance alongside financing. Additionally, as a nonprofit, they can offer more flexible terms and favorable rates compared to many alternative lenders, with any revenue generated reinvested into their mission of expanding access to capital for underserved entrepreneurs.

Accion Opportunity Fund provides business loans ranging from $5,000 to $250,000 with terms typically between 12 and 60 months, accommodating various business needs from working capital to equipment purchases, inventory expansion, and commercial space improvements. Their application process is designed to be accessible, with loan officers available to provide personalized guidance throughout. While their approval process may take slightly longer than some online lenders (typically 3-5 days), this reflects their thorough but fair evaluation process that considers factors beyond just credit scores, including business cash flow, character, and overall viability. For small business owners seeking not just financing but a true financial partner invested in their success, Accion Opportunity Fund offers a uniquely supportive and mission-aligned alternative to traditional and alternative lending sources.
