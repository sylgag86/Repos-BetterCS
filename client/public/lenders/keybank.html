About KeyBank

KeyBank, with roots dating back to 1825, has grown into one of the nation's largest bank-based financial services companies. As part of KeyCorp, which is headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio, KeyBank serves individuals and businesses through a network of branches and offices across 15 states. The bank has built a solid reputation in business lending, offering a diverse portfolio of financing solutions designed to support businesses at every stage of their development, from startups to established enterprises looking to expand or optimize their operations.

What sets KeyBank apart in the business lending landscape is their relationship-based approach combined with industry-specific expertise. They've organized their commercial banking teams around key industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, real estate, and technology, allowing them to develop deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities in each sector. This industry focus enables KeyBank to provide not just financing, but informed guidance on capital structure and growth strategies tailored to the specific dynamics of a business's market. Their commercial relationship managers serve as strategic advisors who take the time to understand a business's goals and recommend appropriate financial solutions.

KeyBank's business financing offerings include conventional term loans, lines of credit, equipment financing, commercial mortgages, and SBA loans. As an SBA Preferred Lender, they're able to streamline the approval process for SBA loans, making these government-backed financing options more accessible. Beyond lending, KeyBank provides a comprehensive suite of business banking services, including treasury management, merchant services, and international banking capabilities. This holistic approach to financial services allows businesses to consolidate their banking relationship with a single institution that can support their evolving needs as they grow. For businesses seeking a lender that combines the resources of a large national bank with a consultative, relationship-focused approach, KeyBank offers a compelling value proposition in the business lending marketplace.
