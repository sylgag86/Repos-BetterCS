About United Capital Source

Founded in 2011, United Capital Source (UCS) has established itself as a versatile business funding provider that connects small and medium-sized businesses with a variety of financing options tailored to their specific circumstances. Unlike traditional banks that typically offer limited products with rigid qualification requirements, UCS functions as a funding marketplace that works with a network of lenders to provide multiple financing solutions, including merchant cash advances, short and long-term loans, SBA loans, equipment financing, invoice factoring, and business lines of credit. This diversified approach enables them to serve businesses across various industries and financial situations, including those that might not qualify through conventional channels due to less-than-perfect credit or limited time in business.

What distinguishes United Capital Source in the alternative business funding landscape is their consultative approach and focus on speed and flexibility. Rather than pushing businesses toward a single product, UCS assigns each applicant a dedicated funding advisor who works to understand the business's specific needs, cash flow patterns, and growth objectives before recommending appropriate financing structures. Their application process is designed to be straightforward, with minimal documentation requirements compared to traditional bank loans. Decisions are typically provided within 24 hours—significantly faster than conventional financing channels—and approved applicants can receive funding in as little as one business day, addressing the urgent capital needs that many small businesses face.

United Capital Source serves businesses across a wide spectrum of industries, including retail, restaurants, construction, healthcare, transportation, and professional services. Their flexible requirements make them accessible to businesses that might be considered too risky by traditional lenders, with minimum credit scores starting as low as 550 for certain products and funding available for businesses with as little as three months of operating history. They offer funding amounts ranging from $5,000 to $5 million, accommodating both modest capital needs and substantial growth initiatives. For business owners seeking alternatives to traditional bank financing, whether due to credit challenges, time constraints, or specialized funding needs, United Capital Source offers a responsive approach to securing capital with terms that can be tailored to align with the business's cash flow patterns and operational realities.
