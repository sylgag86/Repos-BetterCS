About Nav

Founded in 2012, Nav was established to address a critical gap in the small business ecosystem: the lack of transparency and access to credit information that often prevents entrepreneurs from obtaining the financing they need to grow. The company's founders, who had backgrounds in both consumer credit services and small business operations, recognized that while consumers had numerous resources to understand and improve their personal credit profiles, small business owners lacked similar tools for their business credit. This information asymmetry created significant inefficiencies in the business lending market, with many viable businesses being declined for financing simply because they didn't understand the criteria lenders were using to evaluate them. Nav's founding mission—to bring clarity and accessibility to business credit and financing—represented a novel approach in the financial services industry, shifting from a transaction-focused model to an educational and empowerment platform. Over the years, the company has evolved from a basic credit monitoring service to a comprehensive business financial health platform, incorporating lending marketplace functionality, integrated business management tools, and data-driven insights that help small businesses navigate the increasingly complex financing landscape. Throughout this evolution, Nav has maintained its core commitment to demystifying business credit and financing, positioning itself as an advocate for small business owners rather than merely a service provider.

What distinguishes Nav in the business financial services marketplace is its unique position at the intersection of credit information, financing options, and technology-driven personalization. Unlike traditional credit monitoring services that simply provide raw credit data, Nav translates complex credit information into actionable insights and specific financing recommendations tailored to each business's profile. This approach creates a fundamentally different value proposition from conventional lending marketplaces, which typically focus on generating lead volume without necessarily matching businesses to appropriate financing options. Nav's technology platform analyzes over 2,000 data points across business and personal credit profiles, banking information, industry classification, and other relevant factors to generate highly personalized funding matches with significantly higher approval probabilities than random applications. This targeted matching approach benefits both business owners, who avoid wasting time on applications unlikely to be approved, and lending partners, who receive pre-qualified leads matching their specific criteria. Additionally, Nav's comprehensive view of a business's financial profile enables it to provide recommendations across multiple financing categories—from traditional loans and lines of credit to business credit cards and specialized industry financing—creating a more holistic approach compared to single-product financing providers. For small business owners navigating the often overwhelming landscape of business financing options, Nav's combination of objective guidance, transparent information, and simplified application processes represents a uniquely valuable resource that transforms the traditionally adversarial relationship between businesses and financial services into a collaborative partnership.

Nav's service offering encompasses several interrelated components designed to improve small businesses' financial outcomes. Their core Business Manager platform provides monitoring of both business and personal credit profiles, including reports from all major bureaus (Experian, Equifax, Dun & Bradstreet, and TransUnion), allowing businesses to track their credit scores and identify factors affecting their financing eligibility. For businesses seeking funding, Nav's Marketplace offers a matching service that analyzes the business's profile against requirements from over 110 financing providers, presenting personalized recommendations across multiple categories including term loans, lines of credit, SBA loans, invoice factoring, equipment financing, and business credit cards. Their proprietary MatchFactor™ technology assigns compatibility scores that indicate the likelihood of approval for each financing option, helping businesses focus on the most viable opportunities. Nav offers both free and premium subscription tiers, with the free service providing basic credit monitoring and limited funding matches, while premium plans (ranging from $29.99 to $49.99 monthly) offer comprehensive credit reporting, advanced alerts, additional financing matches, and dedicated support. For businesses actively seeking funding, Nav's platform simplifies the application process by allowing users to securely share their pre-populated information with multiple lenders through a single interface, reducing the typical paperwork burden. Beyond these direct services, Nav provides an extensive educational resource library with guides, webinars, and tools focused on helping businesses build stronger credit profiles, understand financing options, and implement sound financial management practices—resources that benefit even users who aren't immediately seeking financing.
