About Upgrade

Founded in 2017 by former LendingClub CEO Renaud Laplanche, Upgrade has rapidly established itself as a significant player in the fintech lending space, offering a unique blend of personal loans, credit cards, and financial education tools. Unlike traditional lenders that focus exclusively on providing funds, Upgrade has developed a comprehensive approach to consumer finance that combines access to credit with resources designed to help consumers build better financial habits. Their personal loan offering quickly gained traction in the market, particularly for debt consolidation purposes, allowing borrowers to replace high-interest credit card debt with fixed-rate, fixed-term loans that provide a clear path to becoming debt-free. This approach addresses a common challenge in the credit card industry, where revolving credit lines can lead to perpetual debt cycles for many consumers.

What distinguishes Upgrade in the personal lending landscape is their emphasis on responsible borrowing and financial health. Their platform includes free credit monitoring, credit health insights, and educational resources designed to help borrowers understand factors affecting their credit scores and make more informed financial decisions. Additionally, they've created innovative hybrid products like the Upgrade Card, which combines the convenience of a credit card with the structured repayment schedule of a personal loan, further reinforcing their commitment to responsible lending practices. Their technology-driven approach allows for a streamlined application process, typically delivering decisions within minutes and funding within days, while maintaining competitive rates compared to many traditional financial institutions.

Upgrade offers personal loans ranging from $1,000 to $50,000 with terms of 24 to 84 months, serving borrowers across a wide spectrum of credit profiles, though they generally require a minimum credit score of around 620 for approval. Their online application process is straightforward and can be completed in minutes, with pre-qualification available using a soft credit pull that doesn't affect the applicant's credit score. Approval decisions are typically provided within 1-3 days, with funds deposited directly into the borrower's bank account shortly after final approval. For individuals seeking a personal loan from a lender that prioritizes both accessibility and financial wellness, Upgrade offers a tech-forward alternative to traditional banks with a clear focus on helping borrowers improve their financial health beyond just providing access to funds.
