About FigoPay

Established in 2016, FigoPay has emerged as an innovative player in the payment processing sector, offering businesses a modern alternative to traditional payment solutions. The company was founded by a team of fintech veterans who recognized that many existing payment processors were burdened by legacy systems, complex fee structures, and inflexible integration options. FigoPay's business model revolves around providing transparent pricing, developer-friendly integration tools, and responsive customer service—addressing key pain points that businesses often encounter with established payment giants. Their cloud-based payment infrastructure was built from the ground up to be adaptable and scalable, enabling them to serve clients ranging from small e-commerce startups to large multi-channel retailers with equal efficiency. Unlike many payment processors that focus primarily on transaction volume, FigoPay has positioned itself as a true business partner invested in the growth and success of its merchant clients.

What distinguishes FigoPay in the payment processing marketplace is their commitment to technology-forward solutions combined with transparent pricing. Unlike traditional processors that often bury fees in complex statements or charge various hidden costs, FigoPay offers straightforward pricing with clearly defined transaction fees and no long-term contracts. Their API-first approach stands in contrast to many legacy providers, making it significantly easier for businesses to integrate payment processing directly into their existing systems and workflows. Additionally, FigoPay has developed specialized solutions for industry-specific needs, such as recurring billing models for subscription businesses, inventory-linked payment systems for retailers, and secure healthcare payment compliance systems—a level of tailored service not typically found with generic payment providers. Their merchant dashboard provides real-time analytics and reporting tools that help businesses understand their cash flow and transaction patterns, rather than waiting for monthly statements.

FigoPay offers a comprehensive suite of payment processing services adaptable to various business needs. Their core offering includes online payment gateways for e-commerce with fraud detection capabilities, in-person point-of-sale systems compatible with major hardware providers, mobile payment solutions for on-the-go businesses, and recurring billing systems for subscription models. Their pricing structure typically includes a standard transaction fee ranging from 2.4% to 2.9% plus a small per-transaction fee (usually $0.25-$0.30), though custom pricing is available for high-volume merchants. Their approval process is generally quicker than traditional merchant account providers, with most businesses receiving account approval within 1-3 business days after completing the application process. FigoPay requires a minimum credit score of 600 for the business owner, though they also consider other factors such as time in business and processing history. For businesses seeking a modern, transparent payment processing solution with flexible integration options and responsive support, FigoPay offers an appealing alternative to traditional payment processors with their technology-driven approach and merchant-friendly policies.
