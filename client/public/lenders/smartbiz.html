About SmartBiz

Founded in 2009 and headquartered in San Francisco, SmartBiz has established itself as a premier marketplace for small business SBA loans by focusing on the intersection of technology and traditional lending. Unlike direct lenders who fund loans from their own capital, SmartBiz operates as a specialized loan marketplace and facilitator that connects small business owners with SBA-approved banks. Their business model revolves around using proprietary technology to streamline what has traditionally been a complex, paperwork-intensive process for obtaining SBA loans. By digitizing application procedures, automating document collection, and implementing advanced matching algorithms, SmartBiz has successfully reduced the typical SBA loan timeline from months to weeks, making these government-backed loans more accessible to a broader range of small businesses.

What distinguishes SmartBiz in the small business lending landscape is their exclusive focus on simplifying the SBA loan process. While many alternative lenders offer quick funding with minimal paperwork but high interest rates, and traditional banks provide low-interest loans but with lengthy, complex application processes, SmartBiz bridges this gap by offering the best of both worlds. Their platform pre-qualifies applicants through a streamlined process, then matches them with the most appropriate SBA lender from their network of partners, significantly increasing approval chances. This curated matching process considers numerous factors including industry, business age, financial history, and specific lender preferences to optimize the pairing of borrowers with the right banking partners. Additionally, SmartBiz provides dedicated relationship managers who guide business owners through the entire application process, helping them navigate the complexities of SBA requirements—a level of personalized service not typically found in digital lending platforms.

SmartBiz facilitates several loan types through their marketplace, primarily focusing on SBA-backed products. Their most popular offerings include SBA 7(a) loans ranging from $30,000 to $350,000 with terms up to 10 years for working capital and 25 years for commercial real estate. These loans feature competitive interest rates typically ranging from 7-9% (variable based on prime rate plus a spread), significantly lower than many alternative funding sources. While traditional SBA loans can take months to fund, the SmartBiz process typically takes 7-14 days from application to funding for well-qualified borrowers. To qualify, businesses generally need to have been operating for at least 2 years, demonstrate positive cash flow, maintain a personal credit score of 640 or higher, and meet SBA eligibility requirements. For businesses seeking the favorable terms of SBA loans without the traditional administrative burden, SmartBiz provides a modernized approach that maintains the benefits while reducing the complexity and timeframe typically associated with these government-backed financing options.
