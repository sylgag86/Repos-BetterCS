About Fundbox

Founded in 2013, Fundbox has established itself as an innovative fintech company focused on providing small businesses with quick access to working capital. Unlike traditional lenders that rely heavily on credit scores and years of financial history, Fundbox pioneered a technology-driven approach to business financing. Their proprietary AI-based risk assessment technology analyzes a wide range of business data points, enabling them to make credit decisions quickly and fund businesses that might be overlooked by conventional financial institutions. To date, Fundbox has connected with over 325,000 businesses, processed more than $2 billion in credit, and built a network of over 80,000 active customers.

Fundbox offers two primary funding solutions: invoice financing (also known as advances) and lines of credit up to $150,000. Their invoice financing product allows businesses to advance payments on outstanding invoices, addressing the common cash flow gap between delivering goods or services and receiving payment. Their revolving line of credit offers flexibility to draw funds as needed up to an approved limit, with only the drawn amount incurring fees. Both products feature a simple fee structure with weekly repayments over a 12 or 24-week term, and no prepayment penalties for early repayment. What distinguishes Fundbox from many competitors is their streamlined application process—businesses can connect their accounting software or business bank account, receive a credit decision in minutes, and access funds as soon as the next business day.

Fundbox serves a diverse range of small businesses across various industries, with a particular focus on those that might face challenges accessing traditional bank financing due to being early-stage, having limited credit history, or operating in underserved sectors. Their customer base includes service providers, construction companies, manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers. While their financing costs can be higher than traditional bank loans, Fundbox fills an important gap in the market by providing fast, accessible funding with minimal paperwork and a high approval rate compared to conventional lenders. For small businesses facing short-term cash flow challenges or seeking capital for growth opportunities with a quick turnaround, Fundbox offers a modern alternative to the often lengthy and restrictive traditional lending process.
