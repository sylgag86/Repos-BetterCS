About Brex

Founded in 2017, Brex has rapidly established itself as an innovative financial services provider focused on meeting the unique needs of growing businesses, startups, and scale-ups. Unlike traditional financial institutions that often apply consumer banking models to business needs, Brex was built from the ground up specifically for companies, with a deep understanding of their cash flow patterns, growth trajectories, and operational requirements. This focused approach has allowed them to reimagine corporate cards and cash management in ways that address long-standing pain points for business owners and finance teams.

Brex's flagship product is their corporate card, which stands out for its unique underwriting model that doesn't require personal guarantees—a significant departure from industry norms. Instead of evaluating business owners' personal credit scores, Brex considers company cash flow, funding, and potential. This approach makes their cards accessible to promising businesses that might be declined by traditional providers. Their cards come with generous credit limits that scale with business growth, along with a rewards program specifically designed for business spending patterns, offering higher points on categories like software subscriptions, travel, and advertising.

Beyond just providing cards, Brex has built a comprehensive financial platform that includes business cash accounts, expense management software, and bill payment capabilities. Their integrated dashboard gives businesses unprecedented visibility into their spending patterns and cash flow, while automation features eliminate many manual accounting tasks. For venture-backed startups and high-growth companies, Brex offers specialized features like seamless integration with accounting software, custom spending policies, and dedicated support. This holistic approach to business finance—combining spending tools, cash management, and intelligent software—positions Brex as more than just a card provider but as a comprehensive financial operating system for modern businesses.
