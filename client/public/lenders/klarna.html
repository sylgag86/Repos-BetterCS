About Klarna

Founded in Stockholm, Sweden in 2005, Klarna has evolved from a simple payment solution provider into a global fintech powerhouse that serves both consumers and merchants across multiple markets. What began as a mission to make online payments safer and simpler has expanded into a comprehensive suite of financial services that include buy-now-pay-later options, direct payments, post-purchase payments, and merchant financing solutions. With over 150 million active consumers and partnerships with more than 450,000 merchants worldwide, Klarna has established itself as one of the most valuable private fintech companies globally. The company's growth trajectory has been fueled by its unique dual-sided approach to the market—simultaneously building consumer loyalty through user-friendly payment options and shopping experiences while offering merchants powerful tools to increase conversion rates, average order values, and customer acquisition. This two-sided network effect has created a powerful ecosystem that continues to expand as more consumers use Klarna's services to shop with more merchants, creating a virtuous cycle of growth and engagement that distinguishes Klarna from competitors focused solely on either the consumer or merchant side of the transaction.

What sets Klarna apart in the increasingly crowded fintech landscape is its integrated approach to financial services that bridges the gap between traditional banking, payments processing, and e-commerce enablement. Unlike conventional banks that typically offer limited digital capabilities or pure-play payment processors that provide transactional services without broader financial solutions, Klarna has built a comprehensive platform that addresses multiple aspects of both the consumer and merchant financial journey. For consumers, Klarna's "Pay in 4" and "Pay in 30 days" options provide flexible payment solutions without the revolving debt and high interest rates associated with traditional credit cards, while their shopping app creates a personalized discovery experience that helps users find products and deals aligned with their preferences. For merchants, Klarna goes beyond simple payment processing to offer a suite of growth tools including marketing services, customer acquisition channels, financing options for business operations, and detailed consumer insights that help optimize conversion and retention strategies. This holistic approach—combined with a strong emphasis on user experience design and seamless integration with e-commerce platforms—has allowed Klarna to achieve significantly higher engagement metrics than traditional financial service providers, with users opening the Klarna app 8 times more frequently than typical banking apps.

For merchants looking to leverage Klarna's business solutions, the company offers several key services designed to drive growth and improve cash flow. Their core payment solutions allow merchants to offer customers flexible payment options at checkout—including pay later, pay in installments, and direct payment options—which have been proven to increase conversion rates by an average of 30% and boost average order values by 45% according to Klarna's internal data. Beyond payments, Klarna offers merchant financing solutions that provide working capital to qualified businesses, with amounts ranging from $1,000 to $1 million based on the merchant's transaction volume and business performance. These financing options feature transparent pricing with a single fixed fee (typically 3-15% of the borrowed amount depending on risk assessment and term length), no hidden charges, and repayment structures that align with the merchant's cash flow patterns. The application process is straightforward and primarily digital, with decisions typically provided within 1-3 business days based on an analysis of the merchant's Klarna payment processing history, overall business performance, and creditworthiness. Additionally, Klarna's platform offers marketing tools that allow merchants to feature their products in Klarna's shopping app and email campaigns, reaching millions of active Klarna users and creating new customer acquisition channels. For merchants seeking comprehensive growth solutions that combine payments flexibility, working capital access, and marketing reach, Klarna's integrated approach offers unique advantages compared to standalone payment processors or traditional business lenders.
