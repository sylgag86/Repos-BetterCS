About Triton Capital

Triton Capital has established itself as a nimble and responsive alternative lender in the small business financing space. Founded with a mission to simplify access to capital for small and medium-sized businesses, Triton has built its reputation by addressing many of the pain points that business owners typically encounter when seeking funding from traditional financial institutions. Their focus on efficiency, transparency, and service has made them a popular choice particularly among businesses that need capital quickly or those that may not meet the stringent requirements of conventional bank loans.

What distinguishes Triton Capital in the competitive alternative lending marketplace is their dual expertise in equipment financing and working capital solutions. Their equipment financing options are designed to help businesses acquire essential machinery, vehicles, technology, or other equipment without depleting cash reserves. With financing available for both new and used equipment and flexible repayment terms, businesses can maintain operational liquidity while still obtaining the assets they need for growth. Similarly, their working capital solutions provide businesses with the flexibility to address immediate financial needs, whether for inventory purchases, staffing expansions, marketing initiatives, or managing seasonal fluctuations.

Triton's approach to the application and approval process directly addresses the frustrations many business owners experience with traditional lenders. They've streamlined the application to require minimal documentation, utilize technology to expedite underwriting, and provide decisions typically within hours rather than weeks. For approved applicants, funding can often be available in as little as 24 hours—a crucial advantage for businesses facing time-sensitive opportunities or challenges. With loan amounts typically ranging from $10,000 to $500,000 and terms from 12 to 60 months, Triton provides financing solutions that can be tailored to align with a business's specific cash flow patterns and growth trajectory. For small business owners who value speed, simplicity, and service in their financing partner, Triton Capital offers a compelling alternative to traditional lending channels.
