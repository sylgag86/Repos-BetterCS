About Chase Bank

Chase Bank, the consumer and commercial banking division of JPMorgan Chase & Co., has established itself as one of the nation's premier providers of business funding solutions. With a history dating back over 200 years, Chase combines financial stability with innovative lending approaches to serve businesses across the United States.

Chase's business loan offerings are particularly notable for their competitive rates and flexible terms. As an SBA Preferred Lender, Chase has streamlined the application process for government-backed loans, making it easier for small businesses to access crucial funding. Their business loan portfolio includes term loans, lines of credit, equipment financing, and specialized SBA loan products.

What sets Chase apart is their personalized approach to business lending. Each client is paired with a dedicated business banker who takes the time to understand the company's unique needs and goals. This consultative approach ensures that businesses receive tailored financial solutions rather than one-size-fits-all products. With over 4,700 branches nationwide and robust online banking capabilities, Chase provides unmatched convenience for business borrowers seeking reliable funding sources.
