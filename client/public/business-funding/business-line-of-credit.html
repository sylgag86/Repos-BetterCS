Business Lines of Credit

A revolving credit line gives your business flexible access to capital for inventory, payroll gaps, and seasonal cash flow needs.

Pros

  • Pay interest only on funds used
  • Reusable credit as you repay draws
  • Faster access than traditional term loans
  • Great for recurring short-term expenses

Cons

  • Rates may be variable
  • Lower limits for newer businesses
  • Can include draw or maintenance fees
  • Overuse can strain cash flow discipline
