Accessibility Statement

Better Capital Solutions is committed to making our website accessible and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.

We work to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and improve accessibility as our platform evolves.

Our ongoing efforts include improving keyboard navigation, readable color contrast, semantic headings, alt text for meaningful images, and mobile usability.

If you experience accessibility barriers on our website, we want to hear from you. Please contact us with the page URL and issue details.
