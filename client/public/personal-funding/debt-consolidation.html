Debt Consolidation Loans

Combine multiple balances into one monthly payment and potentially lower your overall interest cost.

Common Options

  • Fixed-rate personal loans
  • Balance transfer credit cards
  • Home equity products (where appropriate)

What to Check

  • Total payoff timeline
  • Origination and transfer fees
  • APR after promotional periods
  • No prepayment penalties

Best Practices

  • Avoid adding new debt after consolidation
  • Automate monthly payments
  • Track debt-to-income progress
