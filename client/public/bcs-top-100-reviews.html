Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about choosing the right lender and funding solution.

Better Capital Solutions evaluates lenders based on a comprehensive set of criteria including customer reviews, interest rates and fees, transparency, accessibility, funding speed, customer service quality, and overall user experience. Our team of financial experts conducts in-depth research and regular reassessments to ensure our ratings remain accurate and up-to-date.

Traditional banks typically offer more conservative lending products with stricter qualification requirements but may provide more personalized service through physical branches. Online lenders generally offer streamlined application processes, faster funding times, and more flexible eligibility criteria, often catering to borrowers with less-than-perfect credit. However, they may charge higher interest rates and fees compared to traditional banks.

To improve your chances of loan approval: 1) Check and improve your credit score by paying down existing debt and correcting any errors on your credit report; 2) Prepare complete documentation including proof of income, tax returns, and business plans if applicable; 3) Consider adding a co-signer with strong credit; 4) Reduce your debt-to-income ratio; 5) Apply for loan amounts appropriate for your financial situation; and 6) Research lenders to find those most likely to work with borrowers with your specific financial profile.

Common documents required for loan applications include: identification (driver's license, passport), proof of income (pay stubs, W-2 forms, tax returns), proof of residence (utility bills, lease agreement), bank statements, details of existing debt obligations, and credit history. For business loans, you may also need business licenses, financial statements, business tax returns, profit and loss statements, and a comprehensive business plan. Requirements vary by lender and loan type.

BCS Top 100™ certification is awarded to lenders who consistently demonstrate excellence in customer satisfaction, product quality, transparency, and business practices. Our evaluation process involves quantitative analysis of customer reviews, interest rates, fees, and terms, combined with qualitative assessment by our financial experts. This certification is reassessed annually to ensure continued adherence to our rigorous standards. Lenders cannot pay for certification, maintaining the integrity and impartiality of our reviews.
