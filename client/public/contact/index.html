Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get funding?

Funding timelines vary by lender and product. Some of our partners offer same-day approval and funding within 24-48 hours, while others may take 1-2 weeks for full processing.

Do you guarantee funding approval?

While we cannot guarantee approval, our matching system helps you find lenders most likely to approve your application based on your specific profile and needs.

Is there a fee for using your services?

Our core funding matching services are completely free. We are compensated by our lending partners when you secure funding through our platform.

How do you protect my information?

We use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect your data. We never sell your personal information to third parties and only share necessary details with lenders you're matched with.
